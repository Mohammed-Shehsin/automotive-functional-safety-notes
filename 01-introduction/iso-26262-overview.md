# ISO 26262 Overview

ISO 26262 is a functional safety standard for electrical and electronic systems in road vehicles.

## Why It Matters

Modern vehicles contain many safety-related electronic systems such as braking, steering, airbag, battery management, driver assistance, powertrain, and motor control systems.

## Beginner Engineering View

ISO 26262 is not only about writing safe code. It also covers requirements, system design, hardware design, software design, verification, validation, documentation, traceability, production, and operation.

## Personal Observation

Automotive safety is both a technical and process-driven topic. The technical solution must be supported by clear requirements, reviews, tests, and documentation.
