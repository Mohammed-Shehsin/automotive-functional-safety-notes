# Automotive Safety Features

## Electronic Stability Control
Electronic Stability Control helps prevent skidding or loss of control by adjusting braking and engine power.

## Automatic Crash Notification
Automatic Crash Notification can detect crash events and inform emergency responders. It may use information such as airbag deployment.

## Seat-Belt Pretensioners
Seat-belt pretensioners tighten the seatbelt during or just before a crash to reduce occupant movement.

## Pedestrian Detection
Pedestrian detection systems use sensors and algorithms to detect pedestrians and support driver warning or automatic braking.
