# Safety Lifecycle

The safety lifecycle describes how safety is managed from concept to decommissioning.

## Typical Lifecycle Areas

- Concept phase
- System development
- Hardware development
- Software development
- Production
- Operation
- Service
- Decommissioning

## Engineering Meaning

Safety must be considered from the beginning, not added at the end.

## Recruiter Perspective

A junior embedded engineer who understands the safety lifecycle shows awareness that automotive development is not only coding, but structured engineering.
