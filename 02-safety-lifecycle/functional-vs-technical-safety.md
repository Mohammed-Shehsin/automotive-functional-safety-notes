# Functional Safety vs Technical Safety

## Functional Safety Concept
Defines what safety behavior is required at a system level.

Example: The vehicle shall prevent unintended acceleration.

## Technical Safety Concept
Defines how the safety behavior will be implemented technically.

Example: Use sensor plausibility checks, watchdog monitoring, and safe torque shutdown.

## Embedded Perspective
Functional safety says what must be safe. Technical safety explains how the ECU, software, hardware, and diagnostics achieve it.
