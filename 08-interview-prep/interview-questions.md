# Interview Questions

## What is ISO 26262?
ISO 26262 is an automotive functional safety standard for electrical and electronic systems in road vehicles.

## What is ASIL?
ASIL means Automotive Safety Integrity Level. It classifies risk from ASIL A to ASIL D.

## What is HARA?
HARA means Hazard Analysis and Risk Assessment. It is used to identify hazards, assess risks, and derive safety goals.

## What is the difference between fail-safe and fail-operational?
Fail-safe means the system moves to a safe state after a fault. Fail-operational means the system continues operating in a degraded but safe mode.

## What safety mechanisms can be used in embedded systems?
Examples include watchdogs, diagnostics, redundancy, self-tests, CRC, timeout monitoring, and safe-state outputs.
