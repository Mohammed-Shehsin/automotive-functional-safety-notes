# Beginner-Friendly Explanations

## Functional Safety in One Sentence
Functional safety means preventing unacceptable risk caused by system malfunction.

## ASIL in One Sentence
ASIL describes how strict the safety requirements should be for a function.

## HARA in One Sentence
HARA identifies what can go wrong, how dangerous it is, and what safety level is needed.

## V-Model in One Sentence
The V-Model connects every development step with a matching test step.
