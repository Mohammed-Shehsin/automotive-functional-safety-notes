# Glossary

## ISO 26262
Automotive functional safety standard for electrical and electronic systems in road vehicles.

## Functional Safety
Freedom from unacceptable risk caused by malfunctioning behavior of electrical/electronic systems.

## ASIL
Automotive Safety Integrity Level. Risk classification level from ASIL A to ASIL D.

## HARA
Hazard Analysis and Risk Assessment.

## Fail-Safe
A design approach where the system transitions to a safe state after detecting a fault.

## Fail-Operational
A design approach where the system continues operating in a degraded but safe mode after a fault.

## FMEA
Failure Mode and Effects Analysis.

## FTA
Fault Tree Analysis.

## Diagnostic Coverage
Measure of how effectively faults can be detected by diagnostic mechanisms.

## Safety Goal
Top-level safety requirement derived from hazard analysis.
